Help & Guide

Learn how to use PostHub effectively

✍️ Create a Post

Go to the Create Post section, add your name, email, and write your content. You can also attach an image. Then press the Post button.

👀 View Your Posts

Navigate to My Posts to see all posts you have created. You can edit or delete them from here.

✏️ Edit a Post

Click on the Edit button under a post. Make changes and save them to update your post.

🗑️ Delete a Post

Click on the Delete button under a post. Confirm to permanently remove it.

💡 Tips

  • Keep your posts short and meaningful.
  • Use respectful and professional language.
  • Regularly check your posts in My Posts.
  • Logout when you’re done for better security.
